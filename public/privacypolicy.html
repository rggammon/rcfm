8. Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Squac uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Squac uses cookies to help Squac identify and track visitors, their usage of https://squ.ac, and their website access preferences. Squac visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Squac's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Squac's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Squac's use of cookies.

10. Google Analytics and Azure Application Insights

Elaborating on Website Visitors, we use Google Analytics and Azure Application Insights for aggregated, anonymized website traffic analysis. In order to track your session usage, Google / Azure drops a cookie (_ga / ai_session) with a randomly-generated ClientID in your browser. This ID is anonymized and contains no identifiable information like email, phone number, name, etc. We also send Google / Azure your IP Address. We use these Analytics to track aggregated website behavior, such as what pages you looked at, for how long, and so on. This information is important to us for improving the user experience and determining site effectiveness. If you would like to access what browsing information we have - or ask us to delete any GA data - please delete your _ga cookies, reach out to us via webmaster@squ.ac, and/or install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
